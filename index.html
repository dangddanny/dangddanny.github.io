Who I am

Hi, I'm Danny!

I'm a New York based game designer from Minnesota. I studied at Parsons, where I served as the President of the New School Game Club and my Thesis was featured in the student showcase. His passion is using playful interactions to convey socially impactful messages. He has made games about various social issues including gender identity, body image, and the spread of misinformation. His work has been featured at Games for Change, Indiecade East, and Maker Faire. He is currently a fellow of the Made in NY Fellowship, India China Institute Fellowship, and Parsons Elab Fellowship. His latest projects were funded by NYC Media Lab as part of the Viacom Music + VR
fellowship and Verizon Connected Futures II program.
